    • Ketu Puja: Worship of Ketu (The Shadow Planet)

    Ketu Puja is a sacred Hindu ritual dedicated to Ketu, the shadow planet. It is believed that performing this puja can alleviate the malefic effects of Ketu and bring spiritual growth, wisdom, and liberation.

    What is Ketu Puja?

    Ketu Puja is a ritual performed to worship Ketu, the shadow planet in Vedic astrology. Ketu is considered a malefic planet that is associated with spirituality, liberation, and transformation. Despite its negative influence, Ketu’s worship is believed to bring wisdom, spiritual awakening, and relief from the adverse effects it causes in one’s life.

    Why Do We Perform Ketu Puja?

    • Spiritual Growth: Ketu is believed to help in spiritual awakening and leads individuals on the path of liberation (Moksha).
    • Relieves Malefic Effects: Helps in mitigating the negative effects of Ketu, such as confusion, depression, and isolation.
    • Strengthens the Mind: Helps to develop mental clarity, focus, and inner peace.
    • Overcomes Obstacles: Ketu Puja is believed to remove physical, mental, and spiritual obstacles.
    • Detachment from Material World: Ketu helps in reducing attachment to materialistic desires and promotes self-realization.

    Benefits of Ketu Puja

    • Spiritual Enlightenment: Encourages self-realization and spiritual awakening.
    • Relieves Stress: Helps calm the mind and alleviate anxiety and depression.
    • Overcomes Obstacles: Removes obstacles in life related to health, career, and relationships.
    • Improves Relationships: Reduces misunderstandings and conflicts in relationships and enhances personal harmony.
    • Detachment from Material Desires: Aids in reducing excessive attachment to worldly possessions and encourages spiritual pursuits.

    How to Perform Ketu Puja (Step-by-Step)

    Ketu Puja is best performed on Tuesdays or during the Ketu Kaal (inauspicious period of Ketu). Here’s a step-by-step guide:

    1. Preparation: Select a clean, quiet place. Gather the required items such as a picture or idol of Ketu, incense, flowers, black sesame seeds, mustard oil, and a lamp.
    2. Clean Yourself: Take a bath and wear clean clothes. Dark-colored clothes (especially black or blue) are preferable for honoring Ketu.
    3. Light the Lamp: Light a lamp using mustard oil or ghee as an offering to Ketu.
    4. Chant Ketu Mantras: Chant mantras such as "Om Ketave Namaha" or "Om Rahu Ketave Namaha" 108 times to invoke Ketu’s blessings.
    5. Offer Black Sesame Seeds: Offer black sesame seeds to Ketu, as they are considered very auspicious for mitigating the malefic effects.
    6. Prayers: Pray to Ketu for spiritual growth, liberation, relief from obstacles, and mental peace.
    7. Perform Aarti: Perform Aarti with a lamp, moving it in a circular motion while singing praises to Ketu.
    8. Prasad: Offer prasad such as fruits or sweets after the puja, and distribute it among your family members or devotees as a token of blessings.

    Conclusion

    Ketu Puja is a powerful ritual that can help alleviate the malefic effects of Ketu and promote spiritual growth, mental clarity, and liberation. By regularly performing Ketu Puja, you can navigate the challenges of life with wisdom and peace while progressing on the path of spiritual enlightenment.
