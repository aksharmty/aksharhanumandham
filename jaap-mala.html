How to Use a Mala

To use a mala, hold it in your right hand and start with the bead next to the guru bead. For each bead, recite your mantra. Once you reach the guru bead, turn the mala around without crossing the bead to continue your chanting.

Why Use a Mantra Jaap Mala?
